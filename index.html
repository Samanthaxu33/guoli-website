Who We Are 关于我们

Guo Li International is a professional hauling and excavation company based in Toronto. We provide material delivery, site excavation, debris removal, and seasonal services using 20-yard dump trucks — helping homeowners and contractors get the job done, efficiently and reliably.

With years of hands-on experience and a commitment to clean, safe work, we understand what it takes to support your project from start to finish. Whether it's a residential driveway or a large-scale site prep, we bring the right equipment and the right attitude to every job.

Trusted by builders and homeowners across the GTA, we’re here to move your project forward — one load at a time.

国力国际是一家位于多伦多的专业运输与挖掘公司，使用 20 码自卸卡车提供建材运送、场地挖掘、垃圾清理及季节性运输服务，致力于高效、可靠地完成各类工程。

我们拥有丰富的一线经验，注重施工现场的整洁与安全，深知从项目开始到完成需要哪些支持。无论是住宅车道还是大型工地准备工作，我们都能带来合适的设备与专业的服务态度。

深受大多地区承包商与业主信赖，我们用一车一铲，推动您的工程不断前行。

How We Help 我们的服务

From excavation to delivery — we keep your project moving. 从挖掘到运送，我们让您的工程持续推进。

Asphalt & Concrete Removal沥青 / 水泥拆除

We break and remove hard surfaces like asphalt driveways, concrete pads, and walkways.我们拆除沥青车道、水泥地面、人行道等硬质地面结构。

Interlock & Soil Excavation砖石与泥土挖掘

We excavate interlocking bricks, garden soil, and prepare ground for construction or landscaping.挖掘车道砖石、花园泥土，为施工或园艺做好地面准备。

Material Delivery建材运输

We deliver gravel, sand, soil, and stone in various sizes — fast, clean, and reliable.快速运送各类碎石、沙子、泥土、砖块等建筑材料，干净可靠。

Pool & Pit Digging / Backfilling泳池 / 坑位挖掘与填埋

We dig or fill swimming pools, ponds, and other site structures according to project needs.专业挖掘或填平泳池、水池、地坑等各类结构，按项目需求施工。

Construction Waste Hauling建筑垃圾清运

We haul away broken concrete, soil, asphalt, bricks, and other debris to licensed dump sites.清运拆除后的混凝土、泥土、砖石、沥青等施工废料，并送至合规垃圾场。

Winter Hauling & Surface Clearing冬季清雪与地面清理

We provide snow hauling and surface cleaning services for driveways and job sites during the winter season.冬天提供积雪清运、工地表面杂物与砂石清理等服务。
