Our Services 我们的服务

Solutions for Every Scale of Project
满足各类工程规模的解决方案

From residential sites to large-scale excavations — we deliver with integrity. 从住宅施工到大型挖掘项目，我们始终坚持诚信交付。

Construction Debris Hauling 建筑垃圾清运

Fast, Clean, and Responsible Waste Removal 快速出车 · 清理干净 · 合规处置

We haul away concrete rubble, soil piles, broken bricks, and leftover construction materials. With 20-yard dump trucks, we can handle heavy loads quickly and leave your site clean and ready for next steps. 我们清运各类建筑垃圾，包括混凝土碎块、挖掘废土、砖石残料等。使用 20 码大卡车高效装载与转运，确保施工现场干净有序，为下一阶段工程做好准备。

Asphalt & Concrete Removal 沥青与混凝土清除服务

Safe, Precise, and Efficient Surface Removal 安全 · 精准 · 高效

We specialize in the removal of hard surfaces such as asphalt driveways, concrete pads, and walkways. Using compact and heavy-duty machinery, we ensure minimal disruption to surrounding areas while delivering clean, ready-to-build sites. 我们专业拆除沥青车道、水泥地面、人行道等坚硬表面。配备专业设备，施工高效，尽量减少对周边环境的影响，确保场地整洁平整，为后续工程做好准备。

Interlock & Soil Excavation 砖石与泥土挖掘

Clear Cuts, Clean Lines, and Solid Prep Work 整洁切割 · 平整施工 · 专业准备

Whether you're redoing your garden or preparing for hardscaping, we excavate and remove interlocking bricks, loose soil, and organic debris. Our team ensures precise grading and optimal ground preparation. 我们提供砖石拆除与泥土挖掘服务，适用于庭院重整或地面硬化项目。清除花园土、砖石与杂质，精准找平地面，为后续施工打下坚实基础。

Material Delivery 建筑材料运送服务

On-Time and On-Spec Delivery of Aggregates 准时 · 精准 · 无缝对接施工进度

We deliver a wide range of construction materials including gravel, sand, topsoil, and stones of various sizes. All delivered on schedule with our 20-yard dump trucks, clean and ready to use. 提供各种规格砂石、泥土、石块等建筑材料，配备 20 码自家倾卸卡车，确保材料及时送达、清洁可靠、满足各类施工需求。

Winter Hauling & Surface Clearing 冬季运输与地面清理服务

Seasonal Support for Snow & Sand Cleanup 冬季支援 · 清雪运雪 · 清理杂砂

During the winter months, we help contractors and homeowners by hauling excess snow, and removing scattered gravel or debris from driveways and job sites. 冬季期间，我们协助客户清除积雪、车道砂石与施工残留物。快速响应，保障场地安全、道路畅通，是冬季工程与物业的有力后勤支持。

Pool & Pit Digging / Backfilling 泳池与水坑挖掘 / 回填服务

Custom-Sized Excavation for Pools, Ponds, and Pits 尺寸定制 · 精准挖掘 · 场地恢复整洁

We offer excavation and backfilling for swimming pools, koi ponds, drainage pits, and more. Whether you’re building new or removing old structures, we dig to your specs and restore your site with care and precision. 我们承接各类泳池、水池、排水坑等挖掘与填土服务。无论是新建泳池还是拆除旧结构，我们都按客户要求施工，并在完工后确保地面平整、现场整洁。
